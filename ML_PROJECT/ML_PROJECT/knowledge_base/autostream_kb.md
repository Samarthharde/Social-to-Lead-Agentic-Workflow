# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators. It uses AI to streamline the video production workflow, saving creators hours of manual editing time.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Videos:** 10 videos/month
- **Resolution:** Up to 720p
- **Support:** Standard email support (business hours)
- **Features:**
  - Automated cuts and transitions
  - Basic color correction
  - Background music library (50 tracks)
  - Export to MP4

### Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Videos:** Unlimited videos/month
- **Resolution:** Up to 4K
- **Support:** 24/7 priority support
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Basic
  - AI-generated captions (auto-subtitles)
  - Advanced color grading
  - Background music library (500+ tracks)
  - Multi-platform export (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)
  - Brand kit (logos, intros, outros)
  - Analytics dashboard

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Full refund available within the first 7 days of subscription.
- No refunds after 7 days from the subscription start date.
- Refund requests must be submitted via support@autostream.io.

### Support Policy
- **Basic Plan:** Email support during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm EST).
- **Pro Plan:** 24/7 live chat and priority email support.

### Cancellation Policy
- You can cancel your subscription at any time.
- Access continues until the end of the current billing period.
- No partial refunds for unused days after the 7-day window.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. The price difference is prorated for the remaining billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?
AutoStream offers a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required to start.

### What platforms does AutoStream support for export?
- YouTube
- Instagram (Reels, Stories, Feed)
- TikTok
- Facebook
- Twitter/X
- Direct MP4 download

### Does AutoStream work on mobile?
AutoStream is a web-based platform accessible from any modern browser. A mobile app is currently in beta.

### How does AI captioning work?
The AI captioning feature on the Pro plan automatically transcribes your video audio and generates synced subtitles. Supports 30+ languages.
